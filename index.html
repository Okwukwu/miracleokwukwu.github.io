Global Malaria Burden Analysis (2000–2022)

Analyzed global malaria trends to understand the burden of the disease across regions and over time. The project explored indicators such as malaria incidence, mortality rates, and intervention coverage to identify high-risk countries and progress toward elimination goals. Interactive visualizations were created to compare continents, highlight yearly patterns, and track the impact of control measures such as insecticide-treated nets and antimalarial treatments. The analysis supports evidence-based decision-making for public health planning and resource allocation.

Excel • Power BI • Data Visualization
